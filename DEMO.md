# Demo video script (target: 3:30–4:30)

**Setup before recording:** Use a fresh account seeded with ~5 days of data (log a few days of water/sleep/habits/meals the days before, or backdate via the AI: "I slept 6 hours" on previous days). A populated dashboard demos 10× better than an empty one. Record your phone screen (Android built-in screen recorder) with mic audio ON so judges hear Aurora speak. Quiet room, Do Not Disturb on.

---

## 0:00 — Hook (20s)
Open the app fresh (signed out). Welcome carousel auto-shows.
> "This is Aurora — a health companion that doesn't just collect your data, it understands it. Most health apps are forms with charts. Aurora is someone you talk to. Let me show you."

Swipe through 2–3 onboarding pages slowly (they're pretty — let them breathe).

## 0:20 — Auth + onboarding (35s)
Create an account (email). Walk the 4-step setup quickly, narrating one smart detail:
> "During setup Aurora personalizes everything — based on my weight and activity level it calculated my daily water goal automatically."

## 0:55 — Dashboard (30s)
Land on the dashboard.
> "Everything that matters in one calm screen: an AI insight generated from my actual data every morning, hydration, sleep, habits, nutrition, and my streaks."

Point at the insight card and read it aloud — emphasize it references YOUR real numbers.

## 1:25 — Voice companion: the core (90s — the heart of the demo)
Tap the glowing mic button. The orb appears.

**Turn 1 — action:** say *"I just drank 500 ml of water."*
Aurora replies out loud + a ✓ "Added 500ml of water" chip appears.
Swipe back to the dashboard → **the hydration ring already moved**. Say:
> "I didn't touch a form. Aurora understood, took the action, and the app updated instantly."

**Turn 2 — multi-action:** *"I slept about 7 hours last night and had oatmeal with banana for breakfast."*
Two action chips appear (sleep + meal with estimated calories).

**Turn 3 — agency:** *"Create a habit to meditate every morning."*
Show it appearing in the Habits tab.

**Turn 4 — intelligence:** *"How am I doing this week?"*
Let Aurora's spoken answer play — it cites your real water %, sleep average, habit completion.

## 2:55 — Modules speed-run (45s)
- **Hydration:** tap a quick-add — the bottle's water level rises with a living wave.
- **Sleep:** show the week chart + consistency score.
- **Habits:** complete one with a satisfying tick + streak flame.
- **Nutrition:** type "paneer wrap and a mango shake" → ✨ Estimate macros → AI fills calories/protein/carbs/fat.

## 3:40 — The memory + close (30s)
Open Profile → "What Aurora remembers about you" section.
> "Aurora keeps a memory of my patterns — it genuinely gets to know me. Under the hood: React Native with Expo, Supabase with row-level security, and an agentic GPT pipeline with server-side tools — voice in, action taken, voice out, in seconds."
> "Aurora — understand yourself better every day."

---

## Recording tips
- Practice the voice lines twice; speak clearly and at normal speed (Whisper handles it well).
- If a voice turn ever fails on camera, the keyboard toggle (top-right of companion) is your seamless fallback.
- Keep total length under 5:00 — judges stop watching.
- Screen-record at max brightness; dark UI looks gorgeous on video.
