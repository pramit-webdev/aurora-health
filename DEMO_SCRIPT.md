# Aurora — Demo Video Shooting Script 🎬
**Target length: 4:00–4:30** (brief asks 3–5 min; judges watch many, so stay tight)

## Before you record
- [ ] **Seed historical data** (ask Claude) so trends/streaks/charts look lived-in.
- [ ] **Test Google sign-in once** — if it works, feature it; if not, use email.
- [ ] Phone: **Do Not Disturb ON**, brightness MAX, battery >50%, stable Wi-Fi.
- [ ] Use the phone's built-in **screen recorder with internal audio ON** (so Aurora's voice is captured).
- [ ] Record in **scene segments** (not one take) — a fumble only costs one scene. Stitch in any editor (even CapCut on the phone).
- [ ] When Aurora is speaking, **pause your narration** so her voice is clear.

---

## SCENE 0 — Hook (0:00–0:15)
**ON SCREEN:** App icon tap → the breathing aurora orb on the welcome screen.
**SAY:**
> "Most health apps just collect your data and leave you to figure it out. Aurora is different — it understands your data, coaches you, and you can just *talk* to it. Let me show you."
**TIP:** Energy high. This 15 seconds decides if they keep watching.

---

## SCENE 1 — Onboarding (0:15–0:55)
**ON SCREEN:** Swipe through 2–3 welcome cards → tap **Get started** → **Continue with Google** (or email) → walk the setup wizard quickly.
**SAY:**
> "The whole experience is designed to feel calm and premium. Sign-in is frictionless — and onboarding actually personalizes the app. From my weight and activity, Aurora set my daily water goal automatically. I pick my goals, choose how I want to track, and I'm in."
**TIP:** Don't read every field — keep moving. Pause on the auto-calculated water goal and the goals screen.

---

## SCENE 2 — Dashboard (0:55–1:25)
**ON SCREEN:** Land on the (seeded) dashboard. Slowly scroll top to bottom.
**SAY:**
> "This is home — everything that matters in one calm screen. Right at the top, a daily insight the AI generates from my actual data. Then hydration, sleep, habits, nutrition, and my streaks — all live."
**TIP:** Read the insight card aloud — emphasize it references *real* numbers.

---

## SCENE 3 — 🎙️ Voice Companion (1:25–2:50)  ← THE STAR, give it room
**ON SCREEN:** Tap the glowing mic. The orb appears with sonar rings.

**Turn 1 — action + live update:** say *"I drank 500 millilitres of water."*
→ Aurora replies out loud, green ✓ chip appears. Swipe down to the dashboard — **the hydration ring has already moved.**
**SAY:**
> "I didn't touch a form. Aurora understood, logged it, and the dashboard updated instantly."

**Turn 2 — multi-action:** *"I slept seven hours last night and had oatmeal with a banana for breakfast."*
→ Two ✓ chips (sleep + meal with estimated calories).

**Turn 3 — agency:** *"Create a habit to meditate every morning."*
→ Open the Habits tab briefly to show it appeared.

**Turn 4 — intelligence:** *"How am I doing this week?"*
→ Let Aurora's spoken answer play fully — it cites real numbers.
**SAY (after she finishes):**
> "That's the difference — it's a coach that takes real actions and actually knows my data, not a chatbot."

**TIP:** This scene is why you win. Speak your commands clearly at a normal pace. If a turn fails on camera, the keyboard toggle (top-right) is your silent fallback.

---

## SCENE 4 — Modules quick tour (2:50–3:40)
**ON SCREEN, ~12s each:**
- **Hydration:** tap a quick-add — the water bottle fills with a living wave.
- **Sleep:** show the weekly chart + consistency score.
- **Habits:** tick one complete — satisfying check + streak flame.
- **Nutrition:** type *"paneer wrap and a mango shake"* → ✨ Estimate macros → AI fills calories/protein/carbs/fat.
**SAY:**
> "Each module is fast and a little delightful — the water bottle fills as you drink, sleep trends show your consistency, habits build streaks, and for food I just describe my meal and the AI estimates the macros."

---

## SCENE 5 — Intelligence: trends & memory (3:40–4:15)
**ON SCREEN:** Trends tab (consistency score, charts, achievement badges, Week/Month toggle) → Profile → "What Aurora remembers about you."
**SAY:**
> "Over time Aurora shows your progress — a consistency score, weekly and monthly trends, achievements. And it keeps a memory of your patterns, so it genuinely gets to know you."

---

## SCENE 6 — Close (4:15–4:35)
**ON SCREEN:** Back to the dashboard, or the orb.
**SAY:**
> "Under the hood: React Native and Expo, a Supabase backend with row-level security, and an agentic AI pipeline — voice in, real action taken, voice out, in seconds. This is Aurora. Understand yourself better, every day."

---

### After recording
- Trim dead air, stitch scenes, keep it under 5:00.
- Optional: soft background music at low volume (mute it when Aurora speaks).
- Export 1080p portrait. Done. 🌌
